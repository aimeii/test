Blueprint (In Progress)

There are over 25,000 undergraduate students at UC San Diego. From our user research, we can conclude that 75% of these students have not been able to enroll in a class they planned to take. With our class planning tool, we hope to lower this percentage by providing students with the ability to plan out their classes, and provide the university with a quarter by quarter plan from each student in order to gauge interest in what classes to offer during which quarters.

We are currently in the user research phase of this project. So far, we have collected data from 100+ students and faculty.
